Why GPA?

With GPA - the Graduation Planning App - we aim to make finding your courses as easy as A-B-C. Our app will help you plan your classes by suggesting course sequences, showing your major requirements, and even helping you find more classes that suit your interests. We want to make exploring college courses an easy and enjoyable experience…unlike your finals.
